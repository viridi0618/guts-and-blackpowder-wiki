Francs Guide

Master the in-game currency of Guts and Blackpowder — learn how to earn Francs efficiently and what to spend them on

Currency

What are Francs?

The in-game currency of Guts and Blackpowder

Francs are the premium in-game currency in Guts and Blackpowder. They are earned by completing objectives, surviving waves, and participating in matches. Unlike Robux (Roblox's real-money currency), Francs are earned entirely through gameplay and cannot be purchased directly.

Francs serve as the game's progression economy. They are used to purchase weapon skins, cosmetic items, and at the trader NPC in the Saint Petersburg Holdout map. Earning Francs efficiently is key to unlocking the cosmetic items you want.

The Franc economy is designed to reward skilled play and team contribution. Players who complete objectives, survive longer, and play supportive roles earn more Francs over time.

Optimization

Fastest Way to Get Francs

The most efficient Franc farming strategies

Based on the earning rates above, here are the most efficient strategies for farming Francs:

1. Hardcore Endless — Best Overall

Play Endless mode with the Hardcore modifier enabled. The double Franc multiplier applies to wave bonuses and every 5-wave bonus. A coordinated team reaching wave 25+ on Hardcore Endless can earn 5,000+ Francs per run.

2. Speedrun Objective Maps

Complete shorter Objective maps (Vardohus Fortress, Copenhagen, London) quickly and efficiently. While each individual run pays less than longer maps, the faster completion time means more runs per hour and higher total Francs earned.

3. Saint Petersburg Holdout

Saint Petersburg Holdout mode with Hardcore modifier offers consistent Franc earnings from wave bonuses combined with the trader NPC economy. Surviving all 10 waves on Hardcore provides a substantial payout.

4. Play Support Roles

Playing Surgeon, Officer, or Chaplain earns bonus Francs from heals, buffs, and support actions. These bonuses stack with map completion rewards and wave bonuses, making support classes surprisingly lucrative over time.

5. High Wave Endless

Pushing to wave 30+ (or even wave 50 for the truly ambitious) provides the single largest Franc payout in the game. The every-5-wave bonus scales well, and surviving longer waves demonstrates mastery of the game.

Shopping

What to Buy with Francs

Best purchases and what to save for

Francs can be spent on a variety of cosmetic items and in-game purchases. Here is what you should prioritize:

Weapon Skins

High

The most popular Franc purchase. Weapon skins change the appearance of your muskets, sabres, and other equipment. Prices vary from 500 to 5,000 Francs depending on rarity.

Character Cosmetics

Medium

Uniform variants, hats, and other cosmetic items allow you to customize your soldier's appearance. These are purely visual and do not affect gameplay.

Saint Petersburg Trader Items

Situational

In Saint Petersburg Holdout mode, a trader NPC sells supplies between waves using Francs. Items include ammunition, healing items, and temporary buffs that can help you survive all 10 waves.

Limited-Time Cosmetics

High (Seasonal)

During seasonal events (Halloween, etc.), exclusive cosmetic items become available for Francs. These are time-limited and may not return, so prioritize them if you want exclusive gear.

Tips

Franc Farming Tips

Pro strategies for maximizing your Franc earnings

  • •
    Always Enable Hardcore — If your team is skilled enough, always enable the Hardcore modifier. The double Franc multiplier effectively means you earn twice as much for the same time investment.
  • •
    Play with a Coordinated Team — Surviving longer in Endless mode and completing Objective maps more reliably means more consistent Franc earnings. Playing with a regular group through Discord or friends list is the most efficient way.
  • •
    Stack Support Bonuses — Playing Surgeon or Chaplain and actively healing teammates provides bonus Francs on top of map completion rewards. A good Surgeon can earn 200-500 bonus Francs per map from heals alone.
  • •
    Focus on Efficiency, Not Glory — A quick, clean completion of Vardohus Fortress on Hardcore (paying 500 Francs) in 15 minutes is more efficient than spending 45 minutes on San Sebastian for 800 Francs. Calculate your Francs-per-minute rate.
  • •
    Save for Seasonal Items — Limited-time seasonal cosmetics are often the most coveted items. Save up Francs in advance of Halloween and other seasonal events so you can purchase exclusive items when they become available.
