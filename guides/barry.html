Barry — Who Is He & Why Does Everyone Love Him?

The truth about the Catacombes de Paris NPC Easter egg, the Vos Enterrement badge, and the community legend

NPC Easter Egg

Who is Barry?

The hidden skeleton NPC in the Catacombes de Paris

Barry is a hidden NPC (non-playable character) Easter egg found deep within the Catacombes de Paris map. He is a skeleton figure discovered in a secluded tomb chamber, armed with a shovel. Internally, the game files refer to him as "WinchGuy" — a reference to his unique equipment.

Important: Barry is NOT a playable class. There is no unlock process, no multi-step puzzle, and no secret character to play as. Barry is purely an atmospheric NPC Easter egg placed by the developers as a fun hidden discovery for players exploring the catacombs.

The rumors about Barry being a "secret unlockable class" are community myths and memes. They originated from players speculating about the mysterious skeleton in the catacombs, and the legend grew from there. The truth is much simpler: Barry is a fun Easter egg, and that is exactly what makes him special.

Location

Where to Find Barry

His location in the Catacombes de Paris map

Barry is located in the Catacombes de Paris Objective map. To find him:

1.

1. Enter the Catacombes

Play the Catacombes de Paris map on Objective mode. Progress through the map until you reach the underground tunnel sections.

2.

2. Explore the Side Passages

The catacombs are a maze of bone-lined tunnels. Barry's tomb is in a side chamber off one of the main passages. Keep an eye out for a small alcove or doorway that leads away from the main objective path.

3.

3. Find the Tomb Chamber

Look for a small chamber containing a skeleton figure. This is Barry. He is typically found lying down or positioned against a wall, with his signature shovel nearby.

4.

4. Interact to Earn the Badge

Approach and interact with Barry's tomb to earn the Vos Enterrement badge. You do not need any special items or keys — just find him and interact.

Tip: The catacombs are dark and disorienting. Bring a torch or stick with teammates who know the map layout. The side chamber is easy to miss if you are rushing through the objective.

Badge

Vos Enterrement Badge

The badge earned by discovering Barry

Vos Enterrement is the badge awarded for finding and interacting with Barry in the Catacombes de Paris. The name "Vos Enterrement" is French for "Your Burial" — a fitting name for a badge earned in the Paris catacombs.

Badge Name:Vos Enterrement
Map:Catacombes de Paris (Objective)
Requirement:Find and interact with Barry's tomb
Difficulty:Exploration — no combat requirement
NPC Name:Barry (WinchGuy)
NPC Type:Easter Egg NPC (not playable)
Community

Why is Barry So Popular?

The legend behind the skeleton NPC

Barry has become one of the most iconic characters in Guts and Blackpowder despite — or perhaps because of — being just a simple NPC Easter egg. Here is why the community loves him:

  • •
    Mystery and Discovery: The catacombs are dark, eerie, and full of bones. Stumbling upon a named skeleton with a shovel in a hidden side chamber is a genuinely surprising and memorable moment for new players. The mystery of 'who is Barry?' sparks curiosity.
  • •
    Community Memes and Myths: The lack of official explanation about Barry allowed the community to create their own lore. Myths about Barry being a secret unlockable class, a developer self-insert, or a reference to something deeper spread through the community, creating an entire subculture of Barry content.
  • •
    The Shovel: Barry's shovel is iconic. In a game full of muskets, sabres, and bayonets, a simple shovel stands out. The internal filename 'WinchGuy' only adds to the mystique — what does a winch have to do with a shovel? The community has had endless fun speculating.
  • •
    A Shared Experience: Finding Barry is a rite of passage for Guts and Blackpowder players. Veterans enjoy guiding new players to his tomb, and the shared experience of discovering the Easter egg creates a sense of community. 'Have you found Barry yet?' is a common question in the Discord.
Fact Check

Debunking the Myths

Setting the record straight about Barry

✕

Barry is a secret playable class

False. Barry is an NPC Easter egg. There is no way to play as Barry. He is a static skeleton with a shovel, not a character with abilities or equipment.

✕

You need to solve a multi-map puzzle to unlock Barry

False. There is no puzzle chain involving San Sebastian, Leipzig, or Copenhagen. Barry is found exclusively in the Catacombes de Paris map. Simply find his tomb and interact with it.

✕

Barry has unique abilities including a shovel weapon

False. Barry is not playable and has no abilities. The shovel is part of his NPC model, not a usable weapon for players.

✕

Runners specifically target Barry players

False. Since Barry is not a playable class, this mechanic does not exist. Runners behave the same way regardless of which class you are playing.
