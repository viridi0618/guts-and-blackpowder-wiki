Tips

Map Selection Tips

Which maps to play based on your experience level

Beginner Friendly

Vardohus Fortress, Copenhagen, London

Straightforward objectives with good defensive positions. Start here to learn the basics.

Intermediate

Kaub, Roscoff, Westminster, Hougoumont (Endless)

More complex objectives with harder enemy waves. Good for practicing team coordination.

Advanced

San Sebastian, Catacombes de Paris, Berezina (Holdout)

Challenging layouts with tough mechanics. Requires experienced teams and good communication.

Expert

Sleepy Hollow, Transylvania (Boss Events)

The hardest content in the game. Requires perfect coordination and class composition.
